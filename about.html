SHOW YOUR rC3

While most people decorate a Christmas tree, "normal" people decorate their assemblys, install blinken lights, prepare their projects and disappear in a flurry of wires.

Since the congress will take place remotely this year due to Covid-19, the living room, the study, the kitchen, the bed, the hackerspace, the train shed, the studio, the green field etc. will have to help out.

Send us pictures [as file] and links to videos of your rC3 home setup, your blinken lights, your projects, installations, activities and anything else you would like to share with others.

Please send your contributions to showyourrc3@posteo.de! Add a short note where you want your contribution to appear on the map. You can do this by sending us the location:
e.g.: Germany, Hamburg OR the geolocation.

Please follow the rC3 photo policy and ask everyone in the video or picture if they agree. Please confirm this in your mail by: “I ASKED EVERYBODY AND EVERYBODY AGREED!”
If you are unsure, don't take pictures of people, but limit yourself to things, or make the faces unrecognicable.
Links and photos will be reviewed and not uploaded if the rC3 photo policy is violated. Be fair to each other and respect the right to anonymity of all life forms.

This project is open source. If you have any ideas for further functions or just want to join the project, please send an email to showyourrc3@posteo.de. We will contact you!

Come join us and participate!
